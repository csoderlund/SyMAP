Ordering wild Prunus yedoensis against Prunus persica

Prunus yedoensis (P.yedo): The draft genome of Prunus yedoensis from NCBI (24-May-25, GCA_005406145.1). The file contained 4,571 scaffolds for a total of 690,105,700 bases. The scaffolds greater than 300,000bp were loaded into SyMAP for a total of 331 scaffolds and 439,695,718 base.

Prunus persica (Peach): The complete genome of Prunus persica from NCBI (24-May-25, GCF_000346465.2). The downloaded files contained 8 chromosomes, 225,694,811 bases and 22,988 genes.

Using SyMAP v5.6.8, the P.yedo draft sequence was aligned to, and ordered against, the completed peach genome. The resulting dot plot is shown below.
